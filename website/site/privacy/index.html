Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-15

Summary

SpotChecker is designed to help parents enforce device rules using Shortcuts automations and optional Screen Time controls. We aim to collect as little data as possible.

Data we collect

  • Account: Sign in with Apple identifier (used to create a parent account).
  • Device enrollment: a device identifier and a pairing token/secret to authenticate policy checks.
  • Policy events: basic timestamps indicating when a device checked its status (e.g. “Checked status”).

Data we do not collect

  • We do not read your messages, photos, contacts, browsing history, or the content of apps.
  • We do not track precise location.

How we use data

  • To link a parent to enrolled child devices.
  • To deliver the latest policy when the Shortcut/App Intent requests it.
  • To show basic recent activity to the parent (status checks).

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share data with service providers necessary to run the app (e.g. hosting).

Contact

If you have questions, email support@spotchecker.app.
