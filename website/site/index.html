Parental controls for hotspot rules.

SpotChecker helps parents enforce connectivity rules using Apple Shortcuts automations and (optionally) Apple Screen Time / Family Controls.

What it does

  • Parent enrolls a child device and configures rules + an enforcement schedule.
  • Parent installs the Shortcut on the child phone and enables automations to regularly check status and enforce policies at the right times.
  • We integrate with Apple Shortcuts to apply actions decided by the parent (such as turning off Personal Hotspot, Wi‑Fi, or Mobile Data).
  • Optionally, we use Apple Screen Time / Family Controls to help block selected apps and reduce tampering.
